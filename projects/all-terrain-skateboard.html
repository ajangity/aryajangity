All-Terrain Electric Skateboard

High-torque electric mobility platform for uneven terrain

Problem Statement

Conventional electric skateboards struggle with torque limitations and mechanical robustness on uneven terrain.

Mechanical Design

Designed a reinforced chassis, drivetrain, and wheel system to support off-road performance.

Power Electronics & Control

Selected motors, ESCs, and battery systems optimized for torque, efficiency, and thermal safety.

Results

Demonstrated stable operation on rough terrain with improved climbing capability and load tolerance.
