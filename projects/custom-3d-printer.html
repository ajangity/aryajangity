Problem Statement

Designing a humanoid hand capable of dexterous manipulation presents challenges in kinematic complexity, sensing fidelity, and control precision.

Mechanical & Kinematic Design

Developed finger linkages and joint layouts inspired by human anatomy to balance dexterity, strength, and compact form factor.

Embedded Control

Integrated actuators and sensors to enable closed-loop finger control and force-aware grasping strategies.

Future Work

Planned extensions include tactile sensing, vision-based grasp planning, and reinforcement-learning-based control methods.
